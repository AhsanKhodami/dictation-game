üìù Custom Word Lists

Creating Your Own Lists

One of the most powerful features is the ability to create custom word lists tailored to your learning needs:

  1. Click the "üìù Add Custom Word List" button
  2. Give your list a descriptive name (e.g., "Medical Terms", "Science Vocabulary")
  3. Add words in any of these formats:
    • Comma separated: apple, banana, cherry, orange
    • Semicolon separated: apple; banana; cherry; orange
    • Tab separated: apple banana cherry orange
    • Line separated: One word per line
  4. Watch the live preview update as you type
  5. Click "Save Word List" to store it permanently
  6. Your new list appears in the dropdown menu automatically!
